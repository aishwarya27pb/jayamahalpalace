About Jayamahal

Our Story

Discover Heritage & Luxury, at Every Turn

For over a decade, Jayamahal Palace has been Bangalore's destination for guests who refuse ordinary. Originally built in 1884 as a royal residence, the palace now stands as a beacon of colonial architecture, heritage culture, and modern hospitality.

Spread across 19 lush acres in the heart of Bangalore, our heritage hotel seamlessly blends the grandeur of the past with the comforts of the present. Here, every stay, every celebration, and every gathering becomes a timeless memory.
