About Jayamahal

Our Story

Celebrating 120 Years of Timeless Heritage

Jayamahal Palace is not a modern hotel dressed in heritage aesthetics. It is the real thing. Built on 19 acres of carefully preserved gardens and colonial-era architecture, Jayamahal stands as a testament to Bangalore's royal past. For over 20+ years, we have hosted celebrations, conferences, retreats, and stays that matter. Every guest who walks through our gates becomes part of our story.

Our palace grounds have hosted nobility, dignitaries, and celebrated Bangalore families. When you stay here, you're not just booking a room—you're becoming part of a legacy. The gardens remember a different era. The architecture whispers stories. Every stone, every arch, every corner carries history.
